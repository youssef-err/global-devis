# How to Make an Invoice That Gets Paid

Creating a professional invoice is an essential skill for freelancers, contractors, and small business owners. Getting this right means you get paid faster, while looking professional in front of your clients.

## 1. Include Your Business Details
Always start by putting your name, business name, address, and contact information at the very top. This is the first thing your client looks at.

## 2. Itemize Your Work
Break down the exact services or products you provided.
Instead of writing "Consulting - $1,000", write:
* UX Audit (2 hours) - $300
* Figma Prototypes (4 hours) - $700

## 3. Set Clear Payment Terms
Don't just write "Due upon receipt". Be specific: "Net 14" implies the payment is due exactly 14 days from the date of issuance.
